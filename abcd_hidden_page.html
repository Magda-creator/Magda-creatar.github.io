Illustrations

Behind The Mask. Podcast Series

A series of illustrations created for a Behind The Mask podcast series. I made 8 illustrations based on the speakers' pictures.  You can see this on many platforms such as Facebook, Linkedin, Spotify and on Transporeon's website.  I kept the semi-realistic style to show the speaker's real facial features.

Web Design

Web design was a big part of my job. I created several banners and graphics for websites for various clients and events. I was also in charge of creating landing pages from scratch based on the client's creative brief. I came up with ideas and proposed them later during an online meeting to align with the client before finalizing the project. I worked with Front-end and Back-end developer, and with other designers as well, to deliver the final project. Each time I listened to the client's needs, and I visually solved the problems.
