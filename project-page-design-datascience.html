data science business card

About


This is a business card for a data scientist. My client reached out to me because he was looking for his first serious job after graduation
 and wanted to make contact with others who have been in this profession for a while. He attended a few workshops and needed to
easily share his contact information and work with potential employers. Putting his Github username and QR code to his LinkedIn was necessary
 so people can easily see his work. 

The project

For the front, I used sharp contrast and one dominant color. Not exactly yellow, but it has more of a gold vibe. To make the back I copied the
 logo and put neurons in the background to make it look finished and put together. The digital representation
 of neurons suits the profession that is data science. 


Thinking process

I associated data science with a digital brain. I created an outline of a brain and added some simplified neurons.
 Then it looked very crowded so I put some space between the hemispheres of the brain. The final logo is not exactly symmetrical, 
thanks to the neurons that are put in random places, and not too complicated, so it looks good even if it's quite small.


Partnership

To show the work on my portfolio I couldn't reveal the real personal data of my client, so for the sake of showing some quality
work here I had to change the name, phone number and so on and put the business card on mockups. Our partnership was successful
 and later I worked with him on the Qubiton project: creating a logo, illustrations, and social media posts.
You can see the project on my portfolio as well.
